Case Study — Glow Bites

Problem: Building a scalable food delivery app that handles complex relationships between restaurants, menus, users, and orders.

Solution: Implemented a clean MVC architecture separating concerns across models, servlets (controllers), and JSP views. Used JDBC connection pooling for efficient database access and session-based authentication for security.

Outcome: A fully functional, production-ready food delivery platform with seamless UX, fast data retrieval, and a maintainable codebase.
